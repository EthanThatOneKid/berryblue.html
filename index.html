About Berryblue 🫐

Berryblue (Blue) is a pop artist from Dallas, Texas who writes, produces, and performs her own songs. After more than a decade at the piano, she now writes, produces, and performs her own music—blending dreamy melodies with candid, emotionally honest storytelling. From TikTok viral moments to sold-out shows, she's on a mission to create music that makes you feel something real. Every song is a piece of her story, and she can't wait to share it with you.

Music

I create pop music that's honest, emotional, and real. Each song tells a story from my life, exploring themes of love, heartbreak, friendship, and self-discovery. My discography includes my debut album "Uh Oh" (2025), the viral EP "GOT ME LIKE" (2023), and hit singles like "Just Friends," "Selfish," and "Enjoy Yourself." My music has resonated with fans across TikTok, Spotify, YouTube, and beyond.
